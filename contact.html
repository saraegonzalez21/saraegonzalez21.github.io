About Me

I've always been interested in food since my first semester freshman year of high school. My first elective was a food preperation class. We learned how to make pancakes from scratch (I'd only ever known pancakes from a box or frozen), cookies, homemade mac and cheese. Yum. My love of cooking only amplified when I moved to New York and cooking my own meals was cheaper than buying a sad salad everyday, womp womp. So here we are, this is a compilation of my favorite receipes - some are of my own creation, others are modified from other sources. I hope you'll join me on this delicious journey.
