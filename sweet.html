Vegan Lemon Cinnamon Scones

These are literally my favorite quick and easy scones to whip up anytime I need something vaguely sweet. This is the first variety of scones I ever tried and I'm excited to share with all of you! Super simple and scones are very forgiving, no need to weigh flour or ensure your oil is the exact right temperature, otherwise the entire baked good will fall apart. Quick. Easy. Simple. My favorite things. So let's get cooking...

Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup nut milk + extra for brushing
  • Zest of 1-2 lemons
  • Juice of 1-2 lemons
      • Preheat oven to 375F & line a baking sheet with parchment paper
      In a bowl whisk together:
    1. Flour
    2. Sugar
    3. Baking Powder
    4. Salt
    5. Cinnamon
      Add coconut oil and cut in with a pastry cutter (or your hands) until the texture is crumbly
      Add:
    1. Nut milk
    2. Zest of lemon(s)
    3. Juice of lemon(s)
      Lightly mix together - do not overmix! It will cause your scone to become chewy
      Transfer dough to parchment paper and pat into two circles 1" high
      Cut into 8 triangular slices per circle (16 total) and move to create space between triangles
      Brush tops and sides with extra nut milk
      Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown (ish)
      ENJOY!
