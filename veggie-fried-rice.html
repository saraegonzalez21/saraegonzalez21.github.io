Veggie Fried Rice

Veggie fried rice people!!! How exciting - honestly I started making this when I was broke and living with four roommates. Again, super quick, super easy, semi healthy - it's the veggies! I eat this as a main dish as I've heard fried rice is not to be eaten as a side. Full dish all day, every day. Feel free to swap in your favorite veggies or whatever you have on hand is totally fine too - feel free to mix it up. So let's get cooking...

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup crimini mushrooms, sliced and halved
  • 1/2 cup carrots, sliced into discs
  • 1/2 cuo corn
  • 3 large handfuls spinach
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 2 eggs, scrambled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
      • Cook rice according to directions - I usually do this beforehand
      Heat olive oil and garlic in a large saucepan
      Chop mushrooms and carrots - add to saucepan
      Add corn - this is where you would add other veggies too
      Add spinach - cook unit greens start to wilt a bit
      Add rice and mix - create a donut like hole in the middle of the pan
      Pour eggs into middle and mix until cooked
      Add soy sauce and sesame oil
      Stir it all up - hit it with some salt and peper
      ENJOY!
